物流仓库管理

发表于 2016-11-26 | 分类于 管理笔记 ， 供应链 |

八大仓储活动

  1. Receive goods.
    • Check the goods against an order and the bill of lading.
    • Check the quantities.
    • Check for damage and fill out damage reports if necessary.
    • Inspect goods if required.
  2. Identify the goods. Appropriate stock-keeping unit SKU number and the quantity received is recorded.
  3. Dispatch goods to storage.
  4. Hold goods.
  5. Pick goods.
  6. Marshal the shipment. Goods making up a single order are brought together and checked for omissions or errors. Order records are updated.
  7. Dispatch the shipment. Orders are packaged, shipping documents prepared, and goods loaded on the right vehicle.
  8. Operate an information system. A record must be maintained for each item in stock showing the quantity on hand, quantity received, quantity issued, and location in the warehouse.

库存运行效率影响因素

  • Cube Utilization and Accessibility
  • Stock location.
  • Order picking and assembly.
  • Packaging.

People always say inventory are problems. Yes I agree, but partially.

Let’s think about twice why we need inventory? Generally, we need inventory simply because we need buffer so that the following activities don’t have to wait that long. Inventory is sort of lubricant connecting manufacturing elements across the whole supply chain, and also it has significant impact on meeting customers’ requirement.

So when people say inventory are problems, actually they mean inventory is a symbol of problems. Inventory itself is not. Having been focusing on the root causes, the roadmap to control inventory is clear.

八大浪费

发表于 2015-12-23 | 分类于 管理笔记 ， 供应链 |

八大浪费来源于JIT生产方式，其含义与日常的浪费不同的是，JIT的八大浪费是指“凡是超出增加产品价值所必需的绝对最少的物料、设备、人力、场地和时间的部分都是浪费”。

细琢磨琢磨，八大浪费的总结很有意思：

  • 人机料法环测是个很神奇的总结，无论质量问题还是浪费，都可以从这个角度入手考虑；
  • 八大浪费是从内部效率的角度出发（即Functional Excellence或Development）成本节约的切入点，除了最简单粗暴的降价，八大浪费为成本节约提供了很好的思路。虽然八大浪费的消除不代表实现成本节约（有时候很难计算），但是它必然代表内部效率的提升，成本节约是必然的；
  • 八大浪费的核心就是设计、质量、过程的平衡，核心的核心是时间的节约。

目标的结构

发表于 2015-12-21 | 分类于 杂谈 ， 方法论 |

Specifically compared with a general goal, what’s the structure of the goal? How does it should look like?

Performance on your roles & responsibility.

See, you have key performance indicators no matter of on your position of your personal, so before you talk,

  • Do you know what will be your focus in the near future? What are your key performance indicators? What do you want to achieve?
  • Do you understand the commodity you are handling? What’s the overall spending profile? What is the commodity structure?

Development Plan

It’s a kind of something called Functional Excellence, your focus here is on the organization or person itself. How to be more adaptive, more effective, more efficient? You need to answer all of these questions when setting a goal especially a development plan goal. Remember, it’s important to achieve something, as well as how you achieve it.

Relationship

Relationship development doesn’t only care about “Do you have a better relationship with partners”, it’s about more. It’s important to answer below questions:

  • Who are my suppliers, customers, partners and competitors?
  • For all stakeholders, do you have a overview picture of them? Do you have a ABCQ hierarchy to classify? Among them who are most important as key stakeholder? Why they are key?
  • Any opportunity to consolidate suppliers/stakeholders?
  • What is your strategy to keep relationship with them?
  • Do you have a KPI assessment system? Practically, do you have a regular review meeting with them to ensure both are in connection with each other?
